My favourite hobby

Playing Chess



My favorite hobby is to play chess. I used to play chess when I am free. I will solve many puzzles daily and will play online games in websites. I used to go classes for chess but due to this pandemic situation I was attending online chess classes. My master Krishnamoorthy sir used to teach me chess.I have started chess when I am studying 2nd class.My first teacher is my father, he taught me about the names of the pieces and how it moves. When I am studying 3rd I have started learning chess from Krishnamoorthy sir. After that I have won many prizes,medals,cups and certificates also.

My inspiration is Viswanathan anand sir. Viswanathan Anand (born 11 December 1969) is an Indian chess grandmaster and former world chess champion. He became the first grandmaster from India in 1988, and is one of the few players to have surpassed an Elo rating of 2800, a feat he first achieved in 2006.
